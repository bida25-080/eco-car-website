OUR HERITAGE

A journey through speed, innovation, and passion.

ENZO FERRARI

"I have never considered myself a manufacturer or a businessman. I am only a dreamer who has been able to make his dreams come true."

Enzo Ferrari

Born in Modena in 1898, Enzo Ferrari founded the Scuderia Ferrari in 1929 and the Ferrari car manufacturing company in 1947. His passion for racing and technical excellence became the DNA of every car bearing his name.

His vision was simple: to build the perfect racing machine. Today, that vision lives on in every hybrid super-car and grand tourer that bears the Cavallino Rampante.

KEY MILESTONES

1947

The first Ferrari, the 125 S, drives out of the Maranello factory gates. It is powered by a V12 engine designed by Gioachino Colombo.

1962

The legendary 250 GTO is released, becoming one of the most sought-after and expensive classic cars in the world.

1987

The Ferrari F40 is launched to celebrate the 40th anniversary. It is the last car personally approved by Enzo Ferrari.

2013

Ferrari unveils LaFerrari, the brand's first hybrid hypercar, combining extreme performance with electric technology.

2022

Ferrari enters a new era with the Purosangue, the first four-door Ferrari that delivers true sports car performance.
